What even is

Hack Club

?

Hack Club is a non-profit network of coding clubs from all around the world. We're the first Hack Club in Vietnam, passionate about Computer Science and building the things we love!

Do I need any

Experience

?

No, not at all! You just need to have a little bit of programming skill before joining NTP Hack Club! We're all here to learn, to develop together!

I don't learn at your school

Can I join

?

Currently, we are based in NTP Junior Highschool in Hue, Vietnam, however all highschool students are welcome to join!

Can I know your club's

Meeting schedule

?

We are likely to have a meet on Friday at 4pm weekly both offline and online!

What is special about

NTP Hack Club

We are planning to have a lot of hackathons, jams and workshop projects! But most important, everyone will get a special gift ;)
